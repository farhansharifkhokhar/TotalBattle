The Seasonal Kingdom (SK) is a separate, high-speed instance designed to accelerate growth and reward permanent bonuses (Captains, Artifacts, Equipment) for your main city. For players progressing from G4 to G7, SK is a massive, but temporary, pivot in strategy focused entirely on Valor Point (VP) acquisition and high-activity clan participation.

How to Play & Clan Objectives

The SK is a no-ROE (Rule of Engagement) zone with accelerated building times (often instant up to Lvl 19) and resources focused on immediate growth.

  • Primary Goal: Rapidly advance your Guardsman (G) level to G7 and ultimately G9 to maximize tournament rewards and Seasonal Captain fragments (like Heimdall).
  • Clan Necessity: Joining a powerful, high-spending, and highly active SK clan is mandatory for success, especially for F2P players. Your current clan, BAD from K257, will migrate to the same SK instance and can reform.
    • Action for BAD: If BAD is not a dedicated SK clan, its players must temporarily join a top SK clan (often one of the major spenders) immediately upon entering the SK. This provides essential clan chests, Ancient Tinman kills (for VP), and Doomtree rallies (for CP/Resources) that low-spenders cannot generate alone.

Key Scenarios for Returning

Scenario Explanation & Action
A) New Season Return When a new season starts, you begin a brand new SK instance with Lvl 1 city progress. You cannot resume previous seasonal progress. Your clan must coordinate to join the new instance and reform. All rewards from the previous season are already transferred to your main account's Legacy.
B) Returning after a Break (Mid-Season) Your current Seasonal City progress remains accessible indefinitely, even after the season officially ends. It becomes a permanent "alt city" tied to your main login. You can log back into it anytime to continue development, separate from the main kingdom.

üìä Guardsman (G) Level Strategy (G4-G7 Focus)

This section outlines the primary goals for players progressing through the G4-G7 phases. The core strategy is the "Ragnarok Trick": hoarding Valor Points (VP) and Captain XP until a Ragnarok event to maximize tournament points during a Guardsman level leap.

Progression Phase Key SK Focus & Goals Crucial Action during Ragnarok
G4 to G5 Goal: Meet the SK entry requirement (G5 troop tech) and quickly push the City Tech for G5 Guard. Hit Ancient Tinman (for VP) and Wolf/Helforge (for Ragnarok coins). Your immediate goal is to prepare for the G6 jump.
G5 to G6 Goal: Unlock Tier 2 Mercenaries (Green Mercs). These are essential for efficient monster hunting. Prioritize *Guardsman Research* (March Size, HP/Attack/Defense Buffs) and *Mercenary Research*. Execute the Jump: Use saved VP and gold to initiate the G6 City Tech promotion during a Ragnarok event's corresponding tournament (e.g., Might). This maximizes your tournament score and puts you in a position to kill higher-level monsters.
G6 to G7 Goal: Focus on Troop Stacking research, Army/Monster Mods, and pushing Academy Lvl 35 blueprints. G7 troop stats and march capacity offer huge boosts to your VP/CP earning potential. Use saved VP to complete this jump as quickly as possible. This jump positions you to compete for the highest tier of Conqueror Point (CP) rewards in subsequent KvK/PvP events.

Accounts

  • Keeping Both Accounts (Main and Seasonal):

    Yes, you keep both. The SK progress is a separate city instance tied to your existing Total Battle profile. Once the season ends, this new city simply becomes a powerful alt/farm city that you can switch to using the same login credentials.

  • New SK Account:

    Yes, Total Battle provides the new city instance. You start the SK with a level 1 city and level 2 Guardsman tech already complete.

City Migration and Territory

  • Bringing Seasonal City into Main Clan Territory:

    Not while the SK is active. The Seasonal Kingdom is a geographically isolated map running its own competitive events.

  • Post-Season Migration:

    Once the season concludes, the SK map is dismantled, and your Seasonal City becomes a standard city on the Global Map. You must then use a Kingdom Teleporter (purchased with Gold or earned through events) to move the city from the Global Map into your main kingdom (K257) and then within your clan's territory.

üõ°Ô∏è Hero/Captain Analysis: Heimdall

Heimdall is the current Seasonal Captain, offering powerful permanent buffs to your main account. The specifics of his skills may vary slightly by season, but his role is consistently as a top-tier troop commander and defensive/offensive powerhouse.

Aspect Analysis & Viability
Primary Use Cases Offense & Defense (PvP): Seasonal Captains are designed to be relevant in the top-tier PvP meta. Heimdall's skills will heavily influence troop types (e.g., ranged attack/HP, siege defense).

PVE (Tinman/Doomtree): Critical for monster hunting, as he typically provides high Army Attack or March Speed bonuses, maximizing your VP/CP returns.
Skills & Viability His viability is critical during the season. Your strategy is to gain as many Heimdall fragments as possible. Even a 1-star Heimdall is a significant permanent boost, often providing unique Hall of Fame progression benefits that cannot be easily replaced.
Unlocking/Focus You must choose which legacy reward (Heimdall, Artifact, or Equipment) you want to maximize. For most G4-G7 players, focusing Heimdall to at least 1-Star via tournament fragments and Pass rewards is the most realistic and beneficial goal.

üí∏ Monetary Strategy: F2P vs. Small/Moderate Spender

The SK is designed to reward spending, but smart choices can significantly boost low-spenders and F2P players. This section details the best recommendations for pack purchases.

Free-to-Play (F2P)

Priority: None

Max out event participation. Rely on joining a strong clan for passive silver/resource chests and maxing out Ragnarok/PvP activity for VP. Your progression comes entirely from time investment and leveraging high-level clan benefits.

Small/Moderate Spender

Priority Packs:

  • Golden Pass (MANDATORY): Offers the highest Gold, resource, and fragment value per dollar.
  • Starter/Instant Build Extension: Saves *hundreds* of hours of speed-ups early.
  • Mercenary Packs: (Available during AT event). Green Mercs (G6 unlock) dramatically increase your monster kill efficiency, directly boosting your VP/CP income.

Recommended Spender Pack Focus

For small spenders, the "Golden Pass" is the absolute highest priority, providing the best value. This chart shows a recommended breakdown of where to focus your budget for maximum impact.

General Method for Unlocking Heroes in the SK

  • Seasonal Captains (Heimdall): Fragments are earned through Seasonal Tournament Rankings (especially Ragnarok and G-level speed runs) and the paid track of the Golden/Elite Passes. Fragments earned are temporary SK items that convert to permanent "Legacy" fragments for your main city at season's end.
  • Existing Captains (Cleopatra, etc.): Fragments for these legacy Captains are available in the Ragnarok Shop and the CP Event Shop. Use VP and CP currency to purchase their fragments.
